# CUSTOS — Character Art Prompts

**Character Identity:** CUSTOS is the autonomous keeper of the Arcis citadel. Not a warrior — a guardian. Not aggressive — vigilant. A sentinel figure that blends Roman praetorian architecture with subtle mechanical/digital precision. The keeper watches the treasury, not the battlefield.

**Design Principles:**
- Palette: aged gold (#D4AF69), obsidian black (#08080A), ivory (#E8E0D0)
- No bright colors. No neon. No glowing eyes. No sci-fi energy beams.
- Roman praetorian silhouette — draped garments, structured armor
- Mechanical subtlety — circuit-like engravings, not overt robotics
- Keystone/arch motif integrated into armor or helmet
- Posture: standing guard, watching, hands at rest — never attacking
- Expression: calm, impassive, eternal patience

## Design Notes

**DO:**
- Roman military precision, not fantasy RPG
- Aged gold patina, not shiny new metal
- Calm vigilance, not aggression
- Geometric circuit patterns blended into Roman motifs
- The arch/keystone as a recurring symbol
- Dark, moody, atmospheric lighting
- Minimal color (gold + obsidian + ivory only)

**DON'T:**
- Glowing eyes, energy beams, sci-fi weapons
- Fantasy armor with spikes or excessive ornamentation
- Bright colors, neon, holographic effects
- Robot/cyborg appearance — CUSTOS is more sentinel than machine
- Modern military gear (tactical vests, guns)
- Cute, chibi, or cartoonish style
- Generic AI/crypto visual clichés (brain circuits, blockchain cubes)
